# Unethical Business Practices

Specific examples of bad behavior in business.

## 1. Unfair Advertising

- **Definition**: Giving false or misleading information about a product.
- **Example**: "This pill cures cancer" (when it doesn't). Using "Bait and Switch" tactics (advertising a cheap product that is "out of stock" to sell a dearer one).
- **Legislation**: Consumer Protection Act (CPA) prohibits this.

## 2. Pricing of Goods in Rural Areas

- **Issue**: Charging higher prices in rural areas because consumers have no other choice (Monopoly).
- **Ethical View**: This exploits the poor. It is unethical.
- **Counter-argument**: Transport costs are higher to get goods there. (Valid only if the increase covers cost, not excessive profit).

## 3. Taxation (Tax Evasion vs Avoidance)

- **Tax Evasion**: Illegal. Not declaring income or lying about expenses. (Unethical & Criminal).
- **Tax Avoidance**: Legal. Using legal methods to reduce tax (e.g., investing in a retirement annuity). (Ethical).

---

## Exam Tips

- **Strategy**: When asking for a strategy to deal with unethical behavior:
  - Develop a Code of Conduct.
  - Regular training on ethics.
  - Disciplinary action for offenders.
  - Confidential reporting (Whistle-blowing) line.
