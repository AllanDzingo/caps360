# Conflict Management

## Causes of Conflict

- Poor communication.
- Limited resources (fighting over budget).
- Different values/beliefs.
- Unfair workload.

## Conflict Resolution Steps

1. **Acknowledge** that there is conflict.
2. **Identify** the cause.
3. **Brainstorm** solutions together.
4. **Select** the best solution.
5. **Implement** and monitor.

## Grievance Procedure

This is the formal process when an employee is unhappy.

1. **Step 1**: Employee speaks to Supervisor verbally.
2. **Step 2**: If not resolved, submit written grievance to Supervisor.
3. **Step 3**: Refer to Department Manager / HR.
4. **Step 4**: Private meeting / Mediation.
5. **Step 5**: If still not resolved -> CCMA.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Conflict vs Grievance**:
  - **Conflict**: A clash between two parties (Argument).
  - **Grievance**: A formal complaint by an employee against the employer.
