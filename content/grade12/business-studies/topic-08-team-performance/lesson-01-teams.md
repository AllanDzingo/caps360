# Successful Teams

## Criteria for Success

1. **Clear Goals**: Everyone must know what the target is.
2. **Open Communication**: Members must feel free to speak without fear.
3. **Mutual Respect**: Respecting differences.
4. **Defined Roles**: Everyone knows what their job is.
5. **Balance of Skills**: A mix of thinkers, doers, and leaders.

## Stages of Team Development (Tuckman)

1. **Forming**: The team meets. Polite, unsure.
2. **Storming**: Conflict arises. Arguments about roles. (Necessary phase).
3. **Norming**: Settling down. Rules are established. Cohesion grows.
4. **Performing**: Working effectively towards the goal.
5. **Adjourning**: The job is done. The team breaks up.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Scenario**: "The team is arguing constantly." -> Determining the stage: **Storming**.
- **Scenario**: "The team members are getting to know each other." -> **Forming**.
