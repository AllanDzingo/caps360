# Total Quality Management (TQM)

An integrated system where *everyone* in the business is responsible for quality.

## Key Elements of TQM

1. **Total Client Satisfaction**: Meeting and exceeding customer expectations.
2. **Continuous Improvement**: Kaizen. Always looking for small ways to do things better.
3. **Top Management Involvement**: Leaders must drive the quality culture.
4. **Employee Involvement**: Quality Circles (Teams meeting to solve quality problems). Training.
5. **Good Supplier Relationships**: You need quality raw materials to make quality products.

## Impact of TQM

- **Pros**: Less waste, higher customer loyalty, better reputation, higher profits.
- **Cons**: Expensive to implement (training), takes time to change culture.

---

## Exam Tips

- **PDCA Cycle**: Plan, Do, Check, Act. This is the heartbeat of TQM.
- **Quality Circles**: Groups of employees who meet regularly to solve problems.
