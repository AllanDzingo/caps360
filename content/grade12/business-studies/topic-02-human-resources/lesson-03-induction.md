# Induction vs Placement

## Placement

- Putting the selected employee into the specific job.
- Ensuring their skills match the job requirements.
- Correct placement = Happy employee + High productivity.

## Induction (Orientation)

The process of introducing the new employee to the business.

### Content of Induction Program

- Introduction to colleagues and mangers.
- Tour of the premises (toilets, canteen, fire exits).
- Explanation of rules and policies.
- Overview of company history and vision.

### Benefits of Good Induction

- Reduces anxiety/stress for the new employee.
- Employee becomes productive faster.
- Reduces staff turnover (they feel welcome).
- Ensures rules are understood immediately.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Scenario**: "John started work but felt lost and left after 2 days."
  - *Problem*: Poor induction.
  - *Solution*: Implement a buddy system and formal tour.
- Do not confuse Induction (Introduction) with Training (Learning skills).
