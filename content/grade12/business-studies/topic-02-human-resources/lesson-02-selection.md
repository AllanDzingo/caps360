# Selection Procedure

After recruitment (attracting applicants), Selection is the process of choosing the *best* one.

## The Steps

1. **Receipt of Applications**: Gather CVs.
2. **Screening**: Sort CVs into "Meet requirements" and "Don't meet".
3. **Shortlisting**: Select top 5-10 candidates.
4. **Interviewing**: Face-to-face assessment.
5. **Testing**: Skills tests, personality tests, medical tests (if relevant).
6. **Reference Checks**: Call previous employers to verify info.
7. **Offer of Employment**: Written offer.

## The Interview

- **Purpose**: To evaluate the candidate's personality and communication skills.
- **Interviewer Role**: Prepare questions, make candidate comfortable, be unbiased.
- **Interviewee Role**: Research the company, dress professionally, ask questions.

## Employment Contract

Legal agreement between employer and employee.

- Must comply with BCEA.
- Includes: Job title, Hours, Salary, Leave, Termination rules.
- Signed by both parties.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Reference Checks**: Very important to specifically check for *character* and verifying *qualifications* (to avoid CV fraud).
- **Legality**: Interviews cannot ask discriminatory questions (e.g., "Are you planning to get pregnant?").
