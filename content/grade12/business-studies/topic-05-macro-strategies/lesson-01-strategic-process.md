# Strategic Management Process

## Hierarchy of Planning

1. **Vision**: The dream. (Where we want to be in future).
2. **Mission**: The purpose. (What we do every day to get there).
3. **Objectives**: Specific goals (SMART goals).
4. **Strategy**: The plan of action.

## The Process

1. **Review Vision/Mission**: Are we still on track?
2. **Environmental Scan**: Analyse internal (SWOT) and external (PESTLE/Porter) environments.
3. **Formulate Strategy**: Choose the best course of action.
4. **Implement Strategy**: Allocate resources and do it.
5. **Evaluate/Control**: Check if it worked.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Vision vs Mission**:
  - *Vision*: "To be the #1 shoe store in Africa." (Future).
  - *Mission*: "We sell high-quality shoes at affordable prices." (Current Action).
