# PESTLE Analysis

Used to analyse the **Macro Environment** (Factors outside the business we cannot control).

## The Factors

1. **Political**: Govt policy, trade agreements, political stability.
2. **Economic**: Inflation, Interest rates, Exchange rates, Unemployment.
3. **Social**: Crime, HIV/AIDS, Poverty, Trends (Fashion).
4. **Technological**: New machinery, AI, Internet speed, Cybercrime.
5. **Legal**: Acts (LRA, BCEA, CPA, NCA).
6. **Environmental**: Pollution, Global warming, Weather patterns.

---

## Strategic Responses

We cannot *control* these factors, but we can *respond* to them.

- **Inflation is high**: Strategy -> Cut costs or increase prices.
- **New Law passed**: Strategy -> Ensure compliance (train staff).
- **Technology change**: Strategy -> Upgrade systems or be left behind.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Identification**: Read the scenario and pick out the factors.
  - "The Rand is weak" -> Economic.
  - "Riots in the street" -> Social (or Political depending on context).
  - "New waste laws" -> Legal (or Environmental).

---

## Quick Check

1. Under which factor does "High Unemployment" fall? (Economic).
2. Under which factor does "Load Shedding" fall? (Technological - infrastructure, or Economic).
3. Can a business control the PESTLE factors? (No).
