# SWOT Analysis

Used to analyse the **Internal** AND **External** environments.

## Internal (Micro Environment) - We CAN Control

1. **Strengths**: Good things we have. (Skilled staff, strong brand, cash reserves).
2. **Weaknesses**: Bad things inside. (Old machinery, lazy staff, poor location).

## External (Market/Macro Environment) - We CANNOT Control

3. **Opportunities**: Chances to grow. (Competitor failing, new market opening, favourable exchange rate).
2. **Threats**: Dangers. (New competitor, change in laws, high inflation).

---

## Application

- **Aim**: Use Strengths to use Opportunities.
- **Aim**: Use Strengths to avoid Threats.
- **Aim**: Fix Weaknesses.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Don't mix them up**:
  - "A competitor opens next door" is a **Threat**, not a Weakness (It's external).
  - "Our marketing is bad" is a **Weakness**, not a Threat (It's internal).

---

## Quick Check

1. Is "High Interest Rates" a Weakness or a Threat? (Threat).
2. Is "Exclusive Patent Rights" a Strength or Opportunity? (Strength).
3. Which two letters of SWOT refer to the Internal environment? (S and W).
