# Problem Solving Cycle

## Problem vs Decision

- **Decision Making**: Choosing between alternatives. (e.g., Buying Machine A or B).
- **Problem Solving**: Process of finding a solution to a difficult issue. (e.g., Sales are dropping).

## The Steps (The Cycle)

1. **Identify the Problem**: Be specific. Not "Sales are bad", but "Sales in KZN branch dropped by 10%".
2. **Define the Problem**: What are the causes?
3. **Formulate Strategy**: Brainstorm solutions.
4. **Implement Strategy**: Put the plan into action.
5. **Evaluate Strategy**: Did it work? If not, restart cycle.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Interviewing**: Often linked to HR. "How can we solve the problem of high staff turnover?"
- **Scenario**: Use the steps to advise a business. "First, identify why staff are leaving..."
