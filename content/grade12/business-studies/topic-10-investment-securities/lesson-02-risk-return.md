# Risk vs Return

The Golden Rule of Investment:
**"The higher the Risk, the higher the Potential Return."**

## Understanding Risk

- **Low Risk**: Bank Deposits, Government Bonds. (Money is safe, but growth is slow).
- **Medium Risk**: Property, Unit Trusts (Balanced).
- **High Risk**: Shares, Venture Capital. (Can lose everything, but can also double money).

## Formula for Return on Investment (ROI)

```
ROI = (Net Profit / Investment Amount) × 100
```

## Simple vs Compound Interest

- **Simple Interest**: Interest calculated only on the original principle.
  - `Interest = P × i × n`
- **Compound Interest**: Interest calculated on Principle + Accumulated Interest.
  - `Amount = P (1 + i)^n`
- **Rule**: Compound interest yields a *much higher* return over time.
