# Management vs Leadership

## Comparison

| Aspect | Leader | Manager |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Focus** | People & Vision | Tasks & Processes |
| **Action** | Inspires & Motivates | Instructs & Controls |
| **Authority** | Earned (Personal influence) | Appointed (Position power) |
| **Change** | Creates change | Manages stability |
| **Risk** | Takes risks | Minimizes risks |

## The "Third" Concept: Entrepreneur

- A leader and manager who also **takes the financial risk** of starting the business.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Scenario**:
  - "She ensures everyone follows the rules" -> **Manager**.
  - "He inspires the team to believe in the future" -> **Leader**.

---

## Quick Check

1. Who focuses on "Systems and Control"? (Manager).
2. Who focuses on "Inspiring People"? (Leader).
3. Can a person be both a Manager and a Leader? (Yes, the best managers are also leaders).
