# Diversity & Inclusivity

## Diversity Issues

Differences among people in the workplace.

1. **Poverty**: Employees may not have transport money or food.
    - *Strategy*: Subsidized meals, transport allowance.
2. **Race/Culture**: Different holidays, languages, beliefs.
    - *Strategy*: Celebrate Heritage Day, allow leave for religious holidays, use English as common language.
3. **Gender**: Women earning less, "Glass Ceiling" (cannot get promoted).
    - *Strategy*: Equal pay for equal work, promote women to management.
4. **Language**: Communication barriers.
    - *Strategy*: Translate documents, use interpreters.
5. **Age**: Gap between young (Tech-savvy) and old (Experienced).
    - *Strategy*: Mentorship programs.
6. **Disability**: Physical barriers.
    - *Strategy*: Ramps, accessible toilets, reasonable accommodation.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Inclusivity**: Means making everyone feel welcome and part of the team.
- **Solution**: Always suggest practical strategies (as above) when given a diversity problem.
