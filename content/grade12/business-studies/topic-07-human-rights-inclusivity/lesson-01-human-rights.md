# Human Rights

Every employee has rights guaranteed by the Constitution.

## Key Rights in the Workplace

1. **Privacy**: Personal information (POPIA) must be protected. No illegal searches.
2. **Dignity**: No bullying or harassment. Evaluation must be respectful.
3. **Equity**: No discrimination based on race, gender, religion. (EEA).
4. **Freedom of Speech**: Right to voice opinions (respectfully).
5. **Safe Environment**: Right to safety gear and safe conditions (OHSA).
6. **Fair Labour Practices**: Right to strike, join a union, fair pay (LRA/BCEA).

---

## Commercial Rights

- **Consumer Rights**: Right to choose, right to safety, right to information (CPA).

---

## Exam Tips

- **Scenario**: "The manager shouted at the cleaner in front of everyone."
  - *Right Violated*: Dignity.
- **Scenario**: "The boss installed cameras in the toilets."
  - *Right Violated*: Privacy.

## Quick Check

1. Which Act protects the right to fair labour practices? (LRA / Constitution).
2. Does an employee have the right to strike? (Yes, if protected).
3. Can an employer search an employee's bag? (Only if it is in the contract/policy and done with dignity).
