# COIDA

## Purpose

- To provide compensation for disablement caused by occupational injuries or diseases sustained or contracted by employees in the course of their employment.
- To provide for death benefits to dependents of employees who die from work-related injuries.

## How it works

1. Employers pay a levy to the **Compensation Fund** annually.
2. The amount depends on the risk of the industry (e.g., Mining vs Office work).
3. If a worker is injured, they claim from the *Fund*, not the *Employer*.

## Benefits

- Medical expenses paid.
- Compensation for temporary or permanent disability (lost wages).
- Death benefits (funeral costs + pension for widow/children).

---

## Impact on Business

| **Positives** | **Negatives** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Protection**: Employers are protected from civil claims (workers cannot sue the employer for injury). | Admin burden (Claiming process). |
| Creates a safer mindset. | Financial Cost (Levies). |

## Exam Tips

- **Exclusions**: Does NOT apply to Military (SANDF) or Police (SAPS) - they have their own funds. Also does not apply to Domestic Workers (Previously excluded, but recent court rulings are changing this - stick to textbook unless updated: *Update: COIDA NOW applies to domestic workers as of recent amendments, check latest CAPS guidelines.* For safety, mention it applies to most formal employees).
