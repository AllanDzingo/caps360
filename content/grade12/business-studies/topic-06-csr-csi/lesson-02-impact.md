# Impact of CSI on the Community

## Positive Impact

1. **Job Creation**: CSI projects (e.g., building a clinic) create jobs.
2. **Skills Development**: Training programs uplift the community.
3. **Better Standard of Living**: Housing, water, and health projects improve lives.
4. **Education**: Bursaries and school support.

## Negative Impact

1. **dependency**: Communities may start relying on the business and stop trying to help themselves.
2. **White Elephants**: Projects that are built but not maintained (e.g., a computer lab with no electricity).
3. **Distribution bias**: Only some people benefit, causing conflict in the community.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Evaluation**: You may be asked to evaluate a CSI project.
  - Ask: Is it sustainable? Does the community actually need it? Is it just for marketing?
