# Debtors Reconciliation

**Purpose**: To check if the **Debtors Control Account** (General Ledger) matches the total of the **Debtors List** (Individual balances).

## Logic

- **Control Account**: Summarises total sales, total receipts. (The "Big Picture").
- **Debtors List**: Adds up what A. Adams, B. Bester, C. Charlie owe. (The "Detail").

## Common Differences

1. **Bad Debts**: Written off in journal but not posted to individual account.
2. **Math Errors**: Control account added up wrongly.
3. **Wrong Columns**: Sales recorded in Debtors Allowances column.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Debtors Allowances**: Reduces the balance (Credit side).
- **Interest Charged**: Increases the balance (Debit side).
- **Dishonoured Cheque (R/D)**: Increases the balance (Debit side) - The debtor owes us the money again.

---

## Quick Check

1. Does "Bad Debts Recovered" affect the Debtors Control Account? (No, it is a separate income account. The debtor's debt was already written off).
2. If the Debtors List total is R50 000 and the Control Account is R50 500, we have a problem. True/False? (True).
3. On which side of the Debtors Control Account do Credit Sales appear? (Debit).
