# Weighted Average Method

**Concept**: We calculate an average price per unit for all stock available for sale.
All units are valued at this single average price.

## Formula (Periodic System)

```text
Weighted Average Price = Total Value of Stock Available ÷ Total Units Available
```

**Components of "Stock Available"**:

- Opening Stock
- (+) Purchases
- (-) Returns
- (+) Carriage on Purchases *(If applicable)*

**Closing Stock Value**:
`Units on Hand × Weighted Average Price`

## Impact of Inflation

- The price smooths out fluctuations.
- Profit is "moderate" (between FIFO and LIFO).

---

## Exam Tips

- **Rounding**: Usually round the price to cents (2 decimals). Check instructions.
- **Returns**: Ensure you deduct returns from BOTH the Total Value and the Total Units.
- **Calculation**:
    1. Sum all Rand values (Open + Purch - Returns).
    2. Sum all Units (Open + Purch - Returns).
    3. Divide Value by Units.
