# Directors & Shareholders

## The Relationship

1. **Shareholders**:
    - The owners.
    - Provide capital.
    - **Risk**: Lose their investment.
    - **Reward**: Dividends + Capital growth.
    - **Power**: Vote at AGM (appoint directors/auditors).

2. **Directors**:
    - The managers.
    - Run the company daily.
    - **Executive Directors**: Work full-time (salary).
    - **Non-Executive Directors**: Attend board meetings only (fees). Independent advice.

## The Annual General Meeting (AGM)

- Held once a year.
- **Agenda items**:
  - Present Audited Financial Statements.
  - Directors' Report (Performance review).
  - Auditor's Report.
  - Re-elect directors.
  - Appoint auditors.
  - Pay dividend.

---

## Exam Tips

- **Why Non-Execs?**: Companies appoint Non-Executive directors to provide *unbiased, independent* advice and to stop Executive directors from paying themselves too much salary.
- **Directors Report**: Written by directors. Tells the "story" of the year. Often optimistic. (Auditor's report is the "truth" check).
